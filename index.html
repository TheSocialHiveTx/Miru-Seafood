A Dining Experience Rooted in the Sea

Miru Seafood brings together the bold flavors of the Gulf Coast and the refined technique of Japanese coastal cuisine. Every dish starts with the finest fresh catch — oysters from Prince Edward Island, Hokkaido scallops, Icelandic cod — and is elevated by house-crafted sauces, sake reductions, and lobster butter finished to order.
